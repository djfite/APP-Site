Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how APP Merchant Services ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Derek Fite in Palm Coast, Florida, collects, uses, and protects information you provide when using our website or contacting us through our AI chat assistant (Ellie) or contact forms.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you submit a quote request, use our chat assistant, or contact us directly:

  • Your name
  • Business name and address
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business type and estimated monthly sales volume
  • Current payment processor (if provided)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your quote request or inquiry
  • Provide information about payment processing and POS solutions
  • Follow up regarding services you have expressed interest in
  • Improve our website and customer experience

3. We Do Not Sell Your Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your information is used exclusively to assist you with payment processing and POS solutions.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party tools to operate our website and collect inquiries:

  • Jotform — used to process contact forms and our AI chat assistant (Ellie). Jotform's Privacy Policy can be found at jotform.com/privacy.
  • Google Analytics (GA4) — used to track anonymous website traffic and usage patterns. No personally identifiable information is collected by Google Analytics.
  • bluu™ / Merrick Bank — our payment processing partner. If you become a merchant, their privacy policy and terms apply to your merchant account.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. Our website is secured with SSL encryption via Cloudflare. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry or provide services. If you would like your information deleted, please contact us and we will honor your request promptly.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of any future communications from us

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business owners and are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
